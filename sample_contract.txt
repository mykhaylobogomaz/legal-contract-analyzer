FRANCHISE AGREEMENT

BETWEEN:  FranchiseCorp International Ltd., a company incorporated under the laws of Delaware
          ("Franchisor")

AND:      The individual or entity named in Schedule A ("Franchisee")

DATE:     As set out in Schedule A

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
RECITALS
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

WHEREAS the Franchisor has developed a proprietary business system, brand, and related
intellectual property (the "System") and desires to grant franchises for the operation of
businesses using the System; and

WHEREAS the Franchisee desires to obtain a franchise for the operation of a franchised
business pursuant to this Agreement on the terms set out herein.

NOW THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants herein and other good and valuable
consideration, the Parties agree as follows:

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
ARTICLE 1 — GRANT OF FRANCHISE
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

1.1  The Franchisor hereby grants to the Franchisee a non-exclusive licence to operate a
     franchised business within an approximate area described in Schedule A (the
     "Territory"). The Franchisee acknowledges that any exclusive territory rights are
     subject to revision at the Franchisor's sole discretion and may be modified,
     reduced, or eliminated by the Franchisor at any time without notice to the Franchisee.

1.2  The Franchisor reserves the right to establish, or to license others to establish,
     additional franchised or company-owned outlets anywhere, including within the
     Territory, in its sole discretion.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
ARTICLE 2 — FEES AND PAYMENTS
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

2.1  Initial Franchise Fee. The Franchisee shall pay a non-refundable initial franchise fee
     of USD 75,000 upon execution of this Agreement. This fee shall be deemed fully earned
     by the Franchisor upon receipt and is non-refundable under any circumstances, including
     termination or rescission of this Agreement.

2.2  Royalty Fee. The Franchisee shall pay a weekly royalty fee equal to nine percent (9%)
     of Gross Sales, as defined herein, without any deduction or set-off whatsoever. The
     Franchisor may, at its sole discretion, increase the royalty fee rate at any time
     without notice by providing written notice via email.

2.3  Marketing Fund. The Franchisee shall contribute a mandatory marketing fund contribution
     equal to four percent (4%) of Gross Sales per week to the Franchisor's national
     marketing fund. The Franchisor shall have sole discretion over the expenditure of all
     marketing fund monies and is under no obligation to account to the Franchisee for
     their use. The marketing fund contributions are non-refundable in all cases.

2.4  Technology Fee. An ongoing technology fee of USD 1,500 per month shall be payable to
     the Franchisor. The Franchisor reserves the right to increase this fee at any time
     without notice.

2.5  All fees are non-refundable once paid. Failure to pay any fee within five (5) days of
     the due date shall entitle the Franchisor to terminate this Agreement immediately and
     without further notice to the Franchisee.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
ARTICLE 3 — TERM AND RENEWAL
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

3.1  Initial Term. This Agreement shall commence on the Commencement Date and continue for
     a period of ten (10) years (the "Initial Term"), unless terminated earlier in
     accordance with this Agreement.

3.2  Automatic Renewal. Upon expiry of the Initial Term, this Agreement shall be subject to
     automatic renewal for successive periods of five (5) years each, unless either party
     provides written notice of non-renewal not less than eighteen (18) months prior to the
     expiry of the then-current term. The terms of any renewal shall be on the Franchisor's
     then-current standard franchise agreement, which may contain materially different and
     less favourable terms than this Agreement.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
ARTICLE 4 — TERMINATION
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

4.1  Termination by Franchisor With Cause. The Franchisor may terminate this Agreement
     immediately upon written notice, without any cure period, if the Franchisee:
     (a) fails to pay any amounts due; (b) fails to maintain quality standards as
     determined by the Franchisor in its sole discretion; or (c) commits any act which
     the Franchisor, in its sole discretion, deems to be detrimental to the System.

4.2  Termination Without Cause. The Franchisor may terminate this Agreement without cause
     upon sixty (60) days' written notice. In the event of termination without cause,
     the Franchisor's liability shall be limited to a refund of the most recent month's
     royalty fee only.

4.3  Effect of Termination. Upon termination or expiry for any reason, the Franchisee
     shall immediately cease all use of the System, the brand, and any intellectual
     property of the Franchisor. All obligations of confidentiality and the non-compete
     restrictions in Article 9 shall survive termination indefinitely.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
ARTICLE 5 — INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

5.1  The Franchisee acknowledges that all intellectual property, including but not limited
     to trademarks, trade secrets, software, data, customer lists, and any improvements or
     modifications created or developed by the Franchisee during the term of this Agreement,
     shall be the exclusive property of the Franchisor and are hereby irrevocably assigned
     to the Franchisor without additional compensation.

5.2  All information regarding the Franchisor's System, operations, business affairs, and
     this Agreement itself is confidential information. The Franchisee shall not disclose
     any confidential information to any third party, including the Franchisee's legal
     counsel, accountants, or lenders, without the prior written consent of the Franchisor.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
ARTICLE 6 — WARRANTIES AND LIABILITY
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

6.1  The System and all support services provided by the Franchisor are provided "as is"
     and without any warranty, express or implied, including any warranty of fitness for
     a particular purpose or merchantability.

6.2  Indemnification. The Franchisee shall defend, indemnify, and hold harmless the
     Franchisor and its officers, directors, agents, and employees from and against any
     and all claims, losses, costs, liabilities, and expenses (including attorneys' fees)
     arising from or relating to the Franchisee's operation of the franchised business,
     even where such claims arise in whole or in part from the Franchisor's own negligence.

6.3  Liquidated damages for breach of this Agreement by the Franchisee shall be assessed
     at an amount equal to the aggregate of all royalty fees that would have been payable
     for the remaining term of the Agreement, discounted at a rate of two percent (2%).

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
ARTICLE 7 — FORCE MAJEURE
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

7.1  The Franchisor shall not be liable for any failure or delay in performance under this
     Agreement due to a force majeure event. Force majeure shall include, without
     limitation, acts of God, pandemics, strikes, government actions, supply chain
     disruptions, or any other cause beyond the Franchisor's reasonable control. The
     Franchisee's obligations to pay all fees shall continue unabated during any
     force majeure event affecting the Franchisor.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
ARTICLE 8 — ASSIGNMENT
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

8.1  The Franchisee shall not assign this Agreement or any interest herein without the
     prior written consent of the Franchisor, which consent may be withheld in the
     Franchisor's sole discretion. The Franchisor may freely assign this Agreement to
     any successor or affiliate without the Franchisee's consent.

8.2  The Franchisee grants the Franchisor a right of first refusal to purchase the
     franchised business at the same price and on the same terms as any bona fide third
     party offer. The Franchisor shall have ninety (90) days to exercise this right.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
ARTICLE 9 — POST-TERM RESTRICTIONS
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

9.1  Non-Compete. For a period of five (5) years following the termination or expiry of
     this Agreement for any reason, the Franchisee shall not, directly or indirectly,
     own, manage, operate, consult for, or be employed by any business that is in any way
     competitive with the System anywhere in the world.

9.2  Non-Solicitation. For a period of five (5) years following termination, the Franchisee
     shall not solicit or employ any employee, agent, or contractor of the Franchisor.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
ARTICLE 10 — DISPUTE RESOLUTION
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

10.1  All disputes arising out of or relating to this Agreement shall be resolved
      exclusively by binding arbitration under the rules of the American Arbitration
      Association, with the seat of arbitration in Wilmington, Delaware. The Franchisee
      expressly waives any right to a jury trial and any right to participate in a class
      action or consolidated arbitration proceeding (class action waiver).

10.2  Governing Law. This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Delaware.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
ARTICLE 11 — GENERAL PROVISIONS
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

11.1  Entire Agreement. This Agreement, together with all Schedules, constitutes the entire
      agreement between the parties with respect to its subject matter and supersedes all
      prior representations, negotiations, and agreements, whether oral or written.

11.2  Waiver. No failure or delay by the Franchisor in exercising any right shall constitute
      a waiver of that right.

11.3  Amendments. The Franchisor may amend this Agreement at any time without notice by
      posting an updated version on the Franchisor's intranet portal. Continued operation
      of the franchised business after such posting shall constitute acceptance.

written above.
